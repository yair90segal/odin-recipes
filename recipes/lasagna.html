Lasagna

Description

This cheese lasgana recipe is an easy vegeterian lasgana with ricotta. maozzarella, and Parmesan chesses. It takes approximately 1 hour in total to make this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasgna noodles
  • 4 cups of ricotta chesse
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 32 ounces spaghetti sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasonings
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and lay noodles flat on foil to cool.
  3. Mix ricotta, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat; add garlic and saut√© 2 minutes. Stir in spaghetti sauce and Italian seasoning until warmed through, stirring occasionally, 2 to 5 minutes.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with a layer of noodles. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta mixture over noodles; top with another layer of noodles. Pour 1 1/2 cups of sauce over noodles and spread the remaining ricotta over the sauce.
  6. Top with remaining noodles and sauce; sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Cover with a greased sheet of foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 45 minutes.
